About

Tyler Sanguinette is currently a Contract Web Designer for New Balance and he was previously an Associate Designer at Grain in Saint Louis, Missouri. As an intern, he worked at Paradowski Creative, Typefounding, Crosspoint Church, and as a Student Assistant Professional in the University of Missouri — St. Louis Human Resources office. With much work experience before graduation, Tyler has experienced projects in web development (responsive), general print media, lettering, printmaking, basic typeface production, and leadership.

Tyler graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts with an emphasis in graphic design at the University of Missouri — St. Louis. He also completed his minor abroad, in art history with emphasis on contemporary museum and gallery studies along with French art and architecture, at the University of East Anglia in Norwich, England.

Proficient in Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, HTML, CSS, SASS, LESS, wireframing, Pages, Keynote, and Numbers. Able to use both web frameworks: Bootstrap and Zurb foundation.
