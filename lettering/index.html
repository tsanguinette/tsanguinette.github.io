Lettering Sketches

Drawing letters can help any designer focus on the core principles of design by helping them measure more accurately by eye, recognizing spacial and systematic relations along with the growth of drawing skills. Keeping a typographic journal helps me continue growing my day to day design skills. These pieces range over the past 3 years from individual letterform experiments to full words.
