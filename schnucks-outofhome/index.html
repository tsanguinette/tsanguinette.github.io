Schnucks Digital Partner Projects

I've created a variety of out of home stadium signage to promote Schnucks in the following sports venues: CITYPARK, Busch Stadium, Enterprise Center, SLU Chafietz Arena, Mizzou Faurot Field, Mizzou Arena, Illinois State Farm Arena, and Illinois Memorial Field. I've also created static and simple animation ads shown on Bally Sports Midwest.

I've created a couple different versions of assets for Schnucks to promote its rewards program, Schnucks Rewards. This included LED fascia boards, Jumbotron, other screens inside the arena as well as concourse TVs.

Before the inaugural 2023 season at CITYPARK, I created LED signage for Schnucks to use at their sponsored promotional games. Since then, I've worked on a few other assets related to STL CITY SC which include video ads for their app and ads for concourse TVs.

During the 2023 St. Louis Billikens basketball season, I created a static video bumper on Bally Sports Midwest to promote Schnucks Rewards Night at the Chafietz Arena. It was shown between stoppage of play and commercials.

During the 2019-2020 St. Louis Blues hockey season and during the COVID-19 pandemic, I created a set of display ads and background for Schnuck Markets to use on the club's website. NHL.com/Blues.

During the 2019 St. Louis Cardinals baseball season, I created a scoreboard ad for Schnuck Markets that promoted its rewards program, Schnucks Rewards. The ad was displayed on the main scoreboard periodically during regular season home games from about mid season to the end of regular season play.
