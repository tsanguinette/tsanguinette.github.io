Schnuck Markets Print

Select print marketing assets from Schnucks.

St. Louis Food and Wine is one of the biggest food and wine shows in the midwest. This event takes place every year at St. Louis Union Station. I created the logo and several supporting pieces which include digital wayfinding maps, the etched wine glass each participant receives, marketing emails, and Schnucks Rewards app marketing cards. The goal of the logo was to make it feel very St. Louis and community focused.

Schnucks created a sweepstakes to give away tickets to the 2020 NHL All-Start game when it was held in St. Louis. I created this logo to help promote the sweepstakes and to use it on other digital marketings assets such as the website, Schnucks Rewards app, and marketing emails.

I designed this logo and in store signage to help promote a sweepstakes between Schnucks and 4 Hands Brewing Co.. It has a gritty feel to it and custom type that alludes to 4 Hands overall aesthetics.

I created this logo to help promote Schnucks’ attempt to break the world’s largest pineapple display. It was used in marketing emails, social media, store signage, other print material and by some local media.

Schnucks Arsenal, in partnership with Fyffe, broke the record for the world’s largest pineapple display at 19,650 pineapples on Friday, June 28, 2019. The display surpassed previous title holders in Virginia, Minnesota, and elsewhere, whose displays included 15,000-18,000 pineapples. A portion of all sales from the display benefited Folds of Honor, a non-profit that provides educational scholarships to the children and spouses of fallen and disabled service members while serving our nation.
